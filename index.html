🔍 How To Recover Your Data

Once you install the software, use these steps to look for your lost files:

💡 Tips For Better Results

Data recovery functions best when you follow certain safety habits. If you lose data, stop saving new files to that drive immediately. New data can overwrite the space that holds your old files, making them impossible to restore. Always save your recovered files to a different physical drive or a separate partition to prevent data loss. If you lose files on an external drive, connect that drive to your computer only after you launch the recovery software.
